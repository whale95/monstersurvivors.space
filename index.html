Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival roguelike where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last. Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters while collecting power-ups and becoming stronger.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience orbs to level up. Choose new abilities and upgrades as you progress to create powerful combinations.

Key Features

Roguelike progression system with permanent upgrades

Multiple character abilities and upgrade combinations

Endless waves of challenging monsters

Dynamic difficulty scaling based on progress

Unique power-up combinations for varied gameplay

Tips for Success

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Focus on a consistent build strategy when choosing upgrades. Prioritize experience collection to stay ahead of the difficulty curve.
